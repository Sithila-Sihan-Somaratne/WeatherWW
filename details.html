WeatherWW's Features and Usage

Features

WeatherWW has a lot of features. Here are some of them:

  • Current Weather: WeatherWW shows the current weather conditions.
  • Forecast: Provides a forecast for upcoming days including predicted temperatures, weather conditions, and chance of precipitation (1‐3 days).
  • Historical Weather: Shows the historical weather (enter a date within the last 7 days, format: YYYY-MM-DD).

Usage

To search for a city's weather details in WeatherWW, you can enter any of the following:

  • Latitude and Longitude: 48.8567,2.3508
  • City Name: Paris
  • US ZIP: 10001
  • UK Postcode: SW1
  • Canada Postal Code: G2J
  • METAR: metar:EGLL
  • IATA: iata:DXB
  • IP Lookup: auto:ip
  • IP Address: 100.0.0.1
